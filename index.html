Welcome

Home n Tech is a company founded by Steve Homen, with over twenty years as a Home Automation Technician. Steve has executed all levels of system building from “rough in” to final “testing and calibration” in both retro and new construction applications.

Steve has worked with several automation systems, Security Camera systems, AV products and music and video servers. Projects have ranged from full home solutions in new builds exceeding ten thousand square feet, to high end theatre and two channel solutions.

With our background we have the knowledge to recommend the correct technology and create the right system for you and your home, from simple media rooms to large scale home automation projects.
